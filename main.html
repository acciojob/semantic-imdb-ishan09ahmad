Movie Summary

The Dark Knight Rises is a superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan and the final chapter of The Dark Knight trilogy. Eight years after the death of Harvey Dent, Bruce Wayne lives in isolation until a new threat appears in Gotham City. The powerful terrorist leader Bane takes control of the city and pushes Batman to his physical and mental limits. Bruce Wayne must return as Batman to save Gotham and protect its people.

Main Actors

Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman
Tom Hardy as Bane
Anne Hathaway as Selina Kyle (Catwoman)
Joseph Gordon-Levitt as John Blake
Gary Oldman as Commissioner Gordon
